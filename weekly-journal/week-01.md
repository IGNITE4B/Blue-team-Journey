# Week 01 — Foundation Start

## Focus

This week I started building the foundation for my transition from physical SOC operations into cybersecurity operations.

My main focus was understanding the structure of the cybersecurity field, setting up my learning environment, and beginning to develop technical and operational thinking.

---

## Topics

- GitHub structure
- SC-900 overview
- Linux basics
- SIEM concepts
- Operational documentation

---

## What I Learned

- Physical security is an important part of the overall cybersecurity ecosystem.
- Many operational concepts from physical SOC environments are transferable to cyber operations.
- Cybersecurity contains multiple specialized areas such as SOC operations, cloud security, detection engineering, governance, and threat hunting.
- Protecting systems also means protecting people, environments, and operational processes.
- Cybersecurity is a rapidly growing field with strong scalability and long-term relevance.

---

## What Confused Me

- Which protocols are most commonly used in cybersecurity environments?
- What are the different types of networks and what are they used for?
